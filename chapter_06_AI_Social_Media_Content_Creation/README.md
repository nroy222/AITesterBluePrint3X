# Chapter 06 — AI Social Media Content Creation

Turn **one idea** into a full publish-ready pack across every platform — without sounding like
a robot, an influencer, or a vendor. These are the fill-in-the-blank templates I use to ship
content for The Testing Academy.

The rule: **plan once, repurpose everywhere.** You don't write seven things. You write one
Hook–Story–Offer, then bend it into seven formats.

---

## The workflow

```
            ┌─────────────────────────────┐
            │  00 — HOOK · STORY · OFFER   │  ← always start here
            │     (plan the idea once)     │
            └──────────────┬──────────────┘
                           │  repurpose into ↓
   ┌──────────┬──────────┬─┴────────┬──────────┬──────────┬──────────┐
   ▼          ▼          ▼          ▼          ▼          ▼          ▼
 YouTube   IG Reel    IG Post   Carousel    Medium      Blog     LinkedIn
  (01)      (02)       (03)       (04)        (05)       (06)       (07)
```

1. Fill out **[00 — Hook · Story · Offer](00_Hook_Story_Offer_Planning.md)** for your idea. This is
   the source of truth. Everything downstream pulls its hook, thesis, and CTA from here.
2. Pick your platforms and open the matching templates.
3. Each template tells you the format, the voice rules, the hook patterns, and gives a
   copy-paste skeleton + a pre-publish checklist.

## Universal voice rules (apply to every template)

- Sound like a **senior colleague over chai** — direct, warm, occasionally blunt. Not an
  influencer, not a vendor, not a lecturer.
- **Open with a personal moment or a blunt/contrarian line** — never a generic stat.
- **Real numbers and specifics** beat vague claims. Never fabricate a statistic — if a claim
  needs a source and you don't have one, cut it.
- **No emojis** (1–2 strategic, almost never).
- **Banned phrases** (never use): *game-changer, leverage, dive deep, in today's fast-paced
  world, it's no secret, unleash, revolutionize, cutting-edge, synergy, supercharge,
  unlock the power.*
- The 3-question sanity check before anything ships:
  1. Does this sound like a senior colleague over chai?
  2. Would a serious professional in my field nod — or wince?
  3. Will my audience actually use this — or is it just clickbait?

  All three must pass.

## How to use these with an AI assistant

Paste the filled-in **00 — Hook · Story · Offer** plus the platform template into Claude / your
AI agent and say: *"Write the [platform] piece using this plan and these template rules. Follow
the format exactly. No banned phrases."* The template is the spec; the plan is the content.
